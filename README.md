## Blue Team SOC Training

As part of my journey in cybersecurity, I have been actively practicing and improving my Blue Team skills through hands-on training exercises on various platforms. These exercises focus on core Security Operations Center (SOC) skills such as log analysis, PCAP file inspection, and Python scripting for incident response and automation. I have completed numerous challenges and labs on platforms like:

- [LetsDefend](https://app.letsdefend.io/training): Realistic SOC analyst scenarios including alert triage, threat hunting, and incident response.
- [Blue Team Labs Online](https://blueteamlabs.online/): Blue Team focused challenges ranging from malware analysis to threat intelligence.
- [CyberDefenders](https://cyberdefenders.org/): Capture-the-flag (CTF)-style challenges that simulate real-world Blue Team tasks.
- [Hack The Box](https://app.hackthebox.com/): Practical labs and SOC-focused modules under the Blue Team training path.

These platforms have allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world simulations, sharpen my detection and response skills, and gain practical experience in defensive cybersecurity.
